### CRITICAL ISSUES (must fix before execution):

1. **Assumption #17 (WRONG): Learning rate for Path B pretraining.** The plan proposes lr=3e-4, but MeCo uses lr=3e-3 for 1.6B models. Using 3e-4 would be 10x too low and waste GPU-hours on slow convergence. **Fix: change to lr=3e-3.**

2. **Assumption #16 (WRONG): Token scale comparison with Tice et al.** The plan claims 2B tokens is "similar scale" to Tice et al., but Tice used ~550B tokens. This mischaracterizes the precedent. The plan should honestly state that 2B tokens is a highly aggressive pilot (0.4% of Tice's budget, 1.25% of MeCo's) with no precedent for being sufficient.

Any difference in EM resistance could be attributed to the cooldown procedure (continued training on different data) rather than URL metadata.
